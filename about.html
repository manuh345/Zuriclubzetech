About ZURI

Discover the story behind Zetech University Research and Innovation Club and our mission to foster the next generation of innovators in Kenya.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ZURI (Zetech University Research and Innovation) emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a collaborative space where students could transform their innovative ideas into tangible solutions that address real-world challenges.

What started as a small group of passionate students has grown into Kenya's most dynamic student-led innovation community, with over 150 active members working across diverse fields including health technology, sustainable development, artificial intelligence, and social innovation.

Today, ZURI stands as a beacon of innovation within Zetech University and the broader Kenyan academic landscape, fostering an ecosystem where creativity meets purpose, and where students are empowered to become the change-makers of tomorrow.
