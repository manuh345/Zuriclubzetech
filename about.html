About ZURI

Discover the story behind Zetech University's premier research and innovation club — and our mission to shape the next generation of African innovators.

Our History

Three years of building something real

2022 — Founding

The Club is Born

Founded under the inaugural leadership of Chairperson Emmanuel Rono, Vice Chairperson Isaac Muendo, Secretary Samuel Oduor, and Treasurer Josephat Onkoba. The founding team established the club's structure, recruited its first members, and organized the initial activities that defined the ZURI mission.

2022 – 2023

Building Momentum

The leadership built momentum — growing community presence, increasing event participation, and formalizing operations. Secretary Samuel Oduor played a pivotal role in maintaining communication and record-keeping as the club rapidly evolved.

2024

Leadership Transition

ZURI's first leadership transition: Isaac Muendo stepped up as Chairperson, Timothy Morangi joined as Vice Chairperson, and Samuel Oduor continued as Secretary. Fresh vision with continuity — expanding ZURI's reach while deepening its community impact.

2025 – Present

New Chapter

Under Moses Tabalya's leadership, the club is now 100+ members strong with ongoing projects in health tech, AI, sustainable tech, and social innovation. Innovation Day 2025 marks a milestone in ZURI's journey.
