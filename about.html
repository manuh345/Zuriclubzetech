About ZURI

Discover the History behind Zetech University Research and Innovation Club and our mission to foster the next generation of innovators in Kenya.

Our History

The club was founded in 2022 with the aim of bringing together like-minded individuals to foster growth, collaboration, and community engagement. Under the inaugural leadership of Chairperson Emmanuel Rono, Vice Chairperson Isaac Muendo, Secretary Samuel Oduor, and Treasurer Josephat Onkoba, the club laid a strong foundation. Their collective efforts focused on establishing the club’s structure, recruiting members, and organizing the first set of activities that would define its mission and values.

Throughout the 2022–2023 term, the leadership worked to build momentum, ensuring that members were actively involved and that the club's vision was clear. The team’s dedication led to a growing presence within the community and increased participation in events. Samuel Oduor, who served as Secretary, played a pivotal role in maintaining effective communication and record-keeping, helping streamline operations as the club evolved

In 2024, the club experienced its first leadership transition. Isaac Muendo, previously the Vice Chairperson, took over as Chairperson, bringing continuity and fresh vision to the role. Timothy Morangi joined the executive team as the new Vice Chairperson, working closely with the returning Secretary, Samuel Oduor. This new leadership aimed to expand the club’s reach and deepen its impact, building upon the groundwork laid by the founding team. As the club moves forward, it continues to grow in strength, guided by a legacy of dedication and collaborative spirit.
