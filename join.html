Why join ZURI?

Here's what you get as a member.

Real project experience

Work on live projects with real users. Build your portfolio with things that actually matter.

Faculty & industry mentorship

Get guided by lecturers and industry professionals who've built and shipped real products.

Hackathons & competitions

Represent Zetech at national and regional competitions. Win prizes, recognition, and job opportunities.

A network that lasts beyond campus

Connect with alumni, industry partners, and fellow innovators across East Africa.

Access to tools & resources

Templates, software licenses, cloud credits, and a curated library of learning materials - all free for members.

Leadership opportunities

Lead project teams, run workshops, and grow into a club leadership role - building skills that go beyond the classroom.
